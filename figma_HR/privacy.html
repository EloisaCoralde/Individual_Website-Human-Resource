Privacy Policy

How PeopleHub collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and messages submitted through our feedback and contact forms, job applications submitted through Careers, and account details (including attendance/time logs) for registered employees.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used to respond to your inquiries, process job applications, manage employee accounts and attendance records, and improve the PeopleHub platform.

3. Who Can See Your Information

Feedback, contact messages, applicant details, and attendance data are only visible to authorized PeopleHub administrators through the internal admin dashboard. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain the information described above for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law.

5. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us through our Contact page.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of PeopleHub after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
